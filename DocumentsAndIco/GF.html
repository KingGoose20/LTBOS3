The Grand Final


Taking place between the 9th and the 11th of October.

With the two top teams seperated by just 1 ladder point, and the third-place team slowly on the come up, the last week of Season 3 LTBO Basketball has a lot to offer.

Schedule


3rd-5th of October

Preparation Week.
Each team and player has complete control over how they spend this week. Whether they want to play, get up shots, or take time off, they are free to do whatever they need in order to prepare for the Grand Finale.


9th of October

The Final Ladder Positioning Decider.
Games will play like usual, but will be best-of-5's (first to 3). Each team's win will reward them one ladder point to decide the final positions. If there is a tie, the tie breaker order is:
- Head-to-head from the day
- Higher for/against from the day
- Higher win-streak from the day
- Team that performed better on the day


10th of October

The Play-In Game.
The second-place team will play the third-place team to decide who will make the championship game. Starting 10 minutes after break starts, and finishing between 5-10 minutes before break ends, the game will go over the entire break. The second-place team will start up 2-0, a reward for placing better during the season. When there is 10 minutes left in the break, the 'goal score' will be the winning team's score, plus 4. This will be the goal for each team, and the first team to hit this goal will win the play-in. If the score is not reached, and the game is forced to finish, the team in the lead will win. If the game is tied, the second-place team will take the win.
Each team will have two timeouts over the break, each lasting 1 minute. They can take the timeout whenever there is a stop in the game, or when their team has the ball. The team GM must be the one to call it. If they call a timeout when they do not have one, they other team will regain possession of the ball, and this will be considered a technical foul (typical technical/unsportsmanlike foul rules apply).


11th of October

The Championship Game.
After a long season of LTBO Basketball, we've reached the championship game. The winner of the play-in game will play the first-place team. The structure of the day will be the same as the play-in game, except both teams will start at 0-0. When there is 10 minutes left in the break, the 'goal score' will be the winning team's score, plus 4. This will be the goal for each team, and the first team to hit this goal will win the play-in. If the score is not reached, and the game is forced to finish, the team in the lead will win. If there is 5 minutes left in the break, and the score is tied, it will be next team to score will win.
Each team will have two timeouts over the break, each lasting 1 minute. They can take the timeout whenever there is a stop in the game, or when their team has the ball. The team GM must be the one to call it. If they call a timeout when they do not have one, they other team will regain possession of the ball, and this will be considered a technical foul (typical technical/unsportsmanlike foul rules apply).
