THE LTBO Visual Database


The LTBO introduces its Visual Database, an easy way to find images and videos of anyone and anything LTBO related.

As a brand new, easily accessible way to view and download any LTBO photographs and videos, the LTBO has created its own Visual Database. Using the link under 'More', or below, anyone is able to find the photos or videos that they require. Every photo from the Grand Finale has been checked, and if a player is visible in the photo, their folder will have that photo. Even if someone is not the 'main subject', if they are in the photo, the photo will be in their folder. A good example of this is image "IMG_9982" in Rudy's folder. While Angus is the main subject of the photo, this photo can still be found in Rudy's folder.

Additionally, all videos from THIS season of LTBO will be stored under the Videos folder. While most videos will be raw, unedited footage, if highlights are clipped they will also be stored there, just like how the photos are.
